Brand Strategy Survey

The Italian Method
for Wellbeing

This questionnaire stems from a co-design process with LAICA's leadership team in Italy. Help us define the strategic direction for the Laica brand.

01 · Objective

The goal of this work was to gather as much information as possible to identify a communication strategy and a storytelling approach capable of seamlessly integrating:

  • the products in the Laica catalogue
  • the new categories currently in development

We were looking for an idea that would highlight every Laica product as part of a single, powerful story.

02 · The Red Thread

We looked for a way to unite our worlds — filtration, vacuum preservation, health — not through the products themselves, but through the meaning they hold for people.
